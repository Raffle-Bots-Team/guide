Edit Raffles

First you need to edit twitter_tags.txt.

Click ADD RAFFLE button and fill in values.

You put the people that you want to tag into it, separate them by spaces or newlines. It doesn't matter whether @ exists or not.

For example, in the following image, whenever you need to tag people, Mass Raffle will randomly choose from abc, hello, Alice, ok, banana, eth, and hello_eth to tag.

Next you need to add raffles. For example, for this raffle:

  • Raffle URL: https://twitter.com/RainbowAlpha_/status/1593118352658280448
  • Follows: @Murasai_NFT,@RainbowAlpha_ (It doesn't matter whether the @ exists or not)
  • Comment: Whatever you want to reply under the tweet. You can use serveral placeholders.
    • {{wallet}}: Will be expanded as your eth wallet address.
    • {{tag_n}}: Will be expanded to tag people in your twitter_tags.txt, where n is a positive integer. For example, {{tag_3}} will tag 3 people.
